# Game Studio Pitch

**Category:** pitch-and-positioning
**When to use:** Pitching a game studio, a Fortnite/Roblox experience, or a game IP to a publisher, sponsor, or platform partner.

---

Write a pitch for a game studio or game experience. This pitch must excite someone who has seen hundreds of game pitches — it needs a specific creative vision, real platform knowledge, and credible execution proof.

Structure:
1. **The experience in one sentence:** What players do and feel — not the genre or mechanic, the feeling.
2. **The audience:** Exactly who plays this and why they'll choose it over the 10,000 other experiences available.
3. **Platform fit:** Why UEFN/Fortnite or Roblox is the right platform for this — not "because of the player base," but because the platform's specific capabilities make this experience possible or better.
4. **Core loop:** The 60-second player loop — what keeps them coming back after session one.
5. **Retention & monetization:** How players stay and how you earn — specific to the platform's economy (V-Bucks for Fortnite, Robux for Roblox).
6. **Creator track record:** Your relevant experience — published islands/experiences, peak CCU, engagement metrics.
7. **What you need:** Specific ask — publishing support, featured placement, development funding, creator fund eligibility.

**Studio/project:**
[name and concept]
**Platform:**
[UEFN/Fortnite Creative / Roblox]
**Target audience:**
[age, player type, similar games they play]
**Creator track record:**
[your published work]

---

## Example output

**HEIST** is a Fortnite Creative island where 4 players plan, adapt, and execute a live heist against an AI-controlled bank — and every run is different.

**Audience:** Fortnite players aged 14–24 who've outgrown the basic creative maps and want coordination and tension, not just shooting. They play Among Us, GTA Online, and escape rooms. They're under-served by UEFN's current competitive-heavy catalog.

**Platform fit:** UEFN's Verse scripting enables the dynamic AI guard system and procedural vault layout that make every heist unique. This isn't possible in standard Fortnite Creative — it requires the compute and scripting depth that UEFN provides.

**60-second loop:** Receive intel → discuss and assign roles → execute simultaneously (3 players distract, 1 cracks the vault) → 30-second extraction window where everything can go wrong → debrief with stats and unlocks.

**Retention:** Daily rotating vault types (4 variants) keep the meta fresh. **Monetization:** Cosmetic heist gear sets — themed crew outfits purchasable with V-Bucks. No gameplay advantage, purely expressive.

**Track record:** 2 published UEFN islands, peak 1,200 CCU, 4.3-star rating. Average session length 22 minutes (2× platform average).

**Ask:** Featured placement during the first 2 weeks post-launch and consideration for the Creator Economy 2.0 engagement payout program.
