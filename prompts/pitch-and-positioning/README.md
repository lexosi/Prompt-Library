# Pitch and Positioning Prompts

**Domain:** Business
**Audience:** Investors, clients, executives, game publishers

## When to Use

Use these prompts when you need to communicate the value of your product, studio, or services in a way that resonates with a specific audience. These are for high-stakes communications where positioning matters.

## Prompts in This Category

| File | Situation |
|------|-----------|
| `ai-product-pitch.md` | Pitching an AI-powered product to investors or early adopters |
| `game-studio-pitch.md` | Pitching a game studio or experience to a publisher or sponsor |
| `freelance-positioning.md` | Positioning yourself as an AI engineering freelancer or consultant |
| `executive-value-pitch.md` | Explaining the ROI of AI engineering investment to a non-technical exec |

## Tips

- Lead with the problem, not the solution — make the audience feel the pain before you offer relief.
- Every pitch needs a specific, credible ask: what do you want the listener to do next?
- Avoid jargon. If you wouldn't say it at dinner, don't put it in a pitch.
