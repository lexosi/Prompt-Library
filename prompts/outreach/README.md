# Outreach Prompts

**Domain:** Business
**Audience:** Potential clients, partners, recruiters, open-source communities

## When to Use

Use these prompts when you need to initiate contact with someone — cold or warm — and need a message that gets opened, read, and replied to.

## Prompts in This Category

| File | Situation |
|------|-----------|
| `cold-outreach-technical.md` | Cold message to a technical leader or potential client |
| `partnership-outreach.md` | Reaching out to a potential technical or business partner |
| `recruiter-response.md` | Responding to a recruiter in a way that sets clear expectations |
| `community-contribution.md` | Introducing yourself or a contribution to a developer community |

## Tips

- Great outreach is specific: prove you've read their work, played their game, or used their product.
- Make the ask frictionless — one clear, low-commitment next step.
- Keep it under 150 words. Longer messages get skimmed or ignored.
