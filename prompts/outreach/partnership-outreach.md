# Partnership Outreach

**Category:** outreach
**When to use:** Reaching out to a potential technical or business partner where both sides would benefit — not a sales pitch, a mutual value proposal.

---

Write a partnership outreach message. The key distinction from sales outreach: you are proposing something where the other party benefits too — you must make that benefit clear and specific, not vague ("synergies").

Rules:
- Start by naming the specific overlap between what they do and what you do
- State the concrete value for THEM first — what they gain, not what you gain
- Include a specific, testable collaboration idea — not "explore how we might work together"
- Keep it under 180 words
- End with a low-commitment next step

**Context:**
- Who I'm reaching out to: [company/person]
- Their relevant work: [what they do that overlaps with mine]
- What I bring: [my specific contribution to a partnership]
- What they gain: [specific benefit for them]
- Collaboration idea: [specific, concrete thing we could do together]
- Channel: [LinkedIn / Email]

---

## Example output

Subject: Fortnite island × your creator tool — a natural fit

Hi [Name],

I've been following [Tool Name] for a few months — your automatic UEFN device documentation generator is exactly what UEFN creators have been asking for in the community forums.

I publish Fortnite Creative islands with a combined 800K+ plays. My audience is exactly the creators who would use your tool, and I'm always looking for tools to feature in my tutorials. I think there's a straightforward collaboration here: I build a short tutorial around your tool for my next island launch, you get direct exposure to 40K creators on my YouTube and Discord.

No contracts or revenue splits needed — just a tool tutorial in exchange for a feature mention in your newsletter. Interested?

[Your name]
